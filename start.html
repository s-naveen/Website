What is Gimme?

Gimme is an FlockOS application that helps teams colloborate with other by getting to know the current events taking place in the world,understanding current financial trends and updating the lexicon of a team's communication. Gimme was built as a part of Flockathon 2017 conducted by HackerEarth.

Why Gimme?

Gimme is a casual form of "give me". The app provides various services such as current news,live scores, live weather updates,live stock market updates and many more in your Flock Chat box itself.

  • Have you ever been at your work place and wondered what is going on around the world. Dont worry as Gimme gives you top news updates in different categories.
  • Wondered about the weather conditions, Gimme gives you the accurate climatic changes about all the cities around the globe.
  • Is your favorite cricket team playing now and you are curious about the scores, Gimme provides you ball-to-ball score updates with precision.
  • Invested in share market and biting your nails about your stocks,Gimme gets you detailed stock values and keeps you blissful.
  • Have a hesitation in use of a particular word, it is time to use Gimme to learn the word along with its usage and update your vocabulary.
