Usage

Getting Started

Go to Flock apps page and install Gimme app.Once the Install is done,you are set to use to use Gimme.

Get News

Gimme sorts various news content according to various categories.To know more news content you can always use the Read more tab at the end of individual news data.

General

To get generalized news about different categories,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news general

Sports

To get news about sports other than cricket,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news sports

Technology

To get news about technology,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news tech

Businesss

To get news about business,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news business

Entertainment

To get news about entertainment,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news entertainment

Games

To get news about games,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news games

Science

To get news about science,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news science

Music

To get news about music,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news music

Cricket

To get news about cricket,use the following command in your chat box.

/gimme news cricket
